Codex pet spritesheets remain the property of their respective users and
creators. The plugin reads them locally and creates a private derivative copy;
it does not redistribute user pet assets.

Milkybyte images under the repository docs directory are creator-authorized
product demonstration screenshots. They are not included in the installable
RedSkill archive and must not be treated as reusable pet source assets.

The earlier sit-pet prototype studied petdex (MIT) as architecture reference.
This no-EXE plugin implementation does not include petdex source code. The
petdex MIT notice remains preserved in the historical project distribution.

The bundled vendor/hatch-pet skill and deterministic spritesheet scripts are
provided under the Apache License 2.0. The full license text is retained at
vendor/hatch-pet/LICENSE.txt.
